    IDEAL FOR EXPATS

    The apartment is situated on the first floor, in a quiet centrally located Neighbourhood of Amsterdam, and is comfortably decorated in a typical 1930s Dutch style, with a natural wooden floor. The bedroom is charming with a large queen-size bed with direct access to the balcony with a nice view. The living room is roomy with a large relaxing sofa, flat-screen TV and dining table. This large sofa can be transformed into a bed for two people.

    The just renovated kitchen is equipped with a coffee maker, electric water boiler, refrigerator, microwave, stove and all regulair kitchenware. The bathroom is fitted with a toilet and shower, sink, mirror, hairdryer, ect. Very fast free Wi-Fi access is available.

    Rembrandt Vondel Apartments is nonsmoking a self-catering accommodations, blankets and linen are provided.

    • The property is 601 m2 appartment from Vondelpark and 2.3 mi from Dam Square.
    • This apartment has a spacious living room, masterbadroom with a balcony, bathroom and hallway.
    • The kitchen is equipped with; a coffee machine, refrigerator, stove and kitchenware.
    • The bathroom is fitted with a shower and toilet.
      • Do you want to feel at home from the minute you arrive in Amsterdam? Then we're your hosts. You're more than welcome to stay at our unique apartment. Just check the listings for availability and send a message when you have any questions, or book instantly.

    Rembrandt Vondel Apartments is very centrally located in the beautiful ‘Old South’, in a very prestigious neighborhood close to the famous Vondelpark and Rembrandtpark.

    • Through the Vondelpark you can access the Museum square with the recently re-opened Rijksmuseum and the van Gogh Museum.
    • Old South is an area with lots of restaurants, shops, supermarkets, gym and famous parks. Plenty of public transport options about 7 minutes walk from the apartment.
    • The famous 'Vondelpark' is a short walk away, as well the museums such as the Rijksmuseum.
    • The tram 2 stops just around the corner and runs continuously and directly to the popular tourist areas including Leidseplein with many restaurants and café’s, Museumplein, shopping street PC Hoofdstraat and Dam Square.
    • Surinameplein is within 601 m of Rembrandt Vondel Apartments and a tram with connections to different districts of Amsterdam departs from there. If you feel like visiting the surroundings, check out Leidseplein (1.6 mi) and Museumplein (1.7 mi). Amsterdam Central Station is 3.1 mi away. Oud Zuid is a great choice for travelers interested in museums, culture and convenient public transportation.
